I am Fashipe Oluwadamilare Ayoola, known in the digital underground as MARCELO YSIM. I am a dedicated Penetration Tester and Cybersecurity student at MIVA Open University, currently honing my craft as a Cloud Administrator Intern at Pintop Technologies Limited, Lagos.
